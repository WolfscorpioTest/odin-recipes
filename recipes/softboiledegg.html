Soft Boiled Egg

Soft boiled eggs are very nutritious. They are great as a meal and a snack!

  • Water
  • Eggs
  • Soy Sauce
  1. Boil water in a pot.
  2. Place eggs into boiling water for 5-6 minutes depending on how gooey you want the center to be.
  3. Cut eggs in half and use soy sauce to dip.
